Mobile Legends

Franco

Frozen Warrior: Franco is a hulking warrior encased in ice, his frozen heart filled with rage. Armed with his mighty anchor, he pulls his enemies in close before crushing them with brute force.

Layla

Malefic Gunner: Layla is a deadly marksman whose accuracy is matched only by her ambition. Armed with her Malefic Cannon, she rains down destruction upon her enemies from afar, making her a formidable force on the battlefield.

Alucard

Demon Hunter: Alucard is a brooding warrior who walks the line between light and darkness. With his cursed sword, he strikes fear into the hearts of his foes, draining their life force with every swing

Miya

Moonlight Archer: Miya is a graceful archer whose arrows never miss their mark. Born under the light of the moon, she draws power from its glow to unleash devastating barrages of arrows upon her enemies.
